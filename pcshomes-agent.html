About Joseph

The agent who knows Fort Drum like a neighbor — because he is one.

I grew up in Copenhagen, NY — right on the Tug Hill — and I now live just outside Fort Drum. That's not a coincidence. I know this region from both sides: the rural North Country where I was raised, and the community that revolves around one of the Army's most important installations. When a family asks me about neighborhoods, winters, schools, or what to expect in this corner of New York, I'm not reading from a script. I actually live it.

As the #1 agent in Jefferson & Lewis County for most homes sold & or volume, I bring more than local knowledge to the table — I bring a track record. I've helped hundreds of military families navigate VA loans, tight PCS timelines, and the unique challenges of buying in Northern New York. I understand what it means to close on a home when your report date is six weeks away. I understand the rental market that kicks in when you get your next set of orders.

I founded PCSHomes because I believe military families deserve a dedicated platform — and a dedicated agent — who understands their life. Not just the transaction, but the move, the timeline, the stress, and the real opportunity that exists at Fort Drum for those who plan ahead.
