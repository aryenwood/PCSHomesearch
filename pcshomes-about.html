Our Story

Built for military families,
by the community that knows them.

PCSHomes connects service members, spouses, and families at Fort Drum with trusted local professionals — so your move doesn't have to feel like you're figuring it out alone.

Why PCSHomes Exists

Every year, thousands of families PCS to Fort Drum
with almost no local guidance.

They land in Watertown with a report date, a Google search, and maybe a Facebook recommendation from someone who left two years ago. They don't know which neighborhoods are 4 minutes from the gate and which are 40. They don't know that two homes on the same street can feed into completely different school districts. And they definitely don't know which contractors will show up on time and which ones won't return a call.

"We watched families overpay for homes because they didn't know the market, get burned by unresponsive vendors, and miss out on their VA benefits because nobody explained them clearly. There had to be a better way."

— PCSHomes Team

PCSHomes was built to fix that. We're a community resource platform — not a brokerage, not a listing site. We vet local professionals across four categories: real estate agents, VA lenders, property inspectors, and home services (movers, cleaners, contractors). Every partner in our network is handpicked for their track record with military families.

And we know that for many families, it's the spouse who carries the weight of the move. Finding the right school. Registering for childcare before the waitlist closes. Unpacking a house alone because their partner is already in the field — or deploying weeks after arrival. PCSHomes exists for the whole family. The service member, the spouse, the kids. If you're making decisions for your family at Fort Drum, this platform is built for you.

When you tell us what you need, we match you with the right expert. You get a warm introduction to someone who already understands military timelines, VA loans, and the Fort Drum market — not a cold call from a random agent who's never heard of BAH.

We also provide the tools and information that every PCSing family needs: BAH calculators, rent vs. buy analysis, school district maps, neighborhood guides, and a PCS checklist built specifically for Fort Drum. All free, all in one place.

Our mission is simple: make every PCS to Fort Drum less stressful, less confusing, and a whole lot smarter.
