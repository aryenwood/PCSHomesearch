## 10. RECOMMENDED APPROACH

1. **Immediately usable (public domain):** Download 3-5 STARBASE photos from DVIDS showing Fort Drum soldiers with local students. These are high-quality, on-brand, and completely free to use.

2. **Immediately usable (free stock):** Download 2-3 warm school imagery photos from Pexels/Unsplash (school bus in snow, school building, children in classroom).

3. **Best district gallery:** Visit the WCSD Photo Gallery (Google Sites) — explicitly says photos can be downloaded and printed for free.

4. **Contact districts:** Email each district's communications office requesting 2-3 photos for a community resource website serving military families. Most school districts are happy to provide photos for positive community use, especially one serving military families.

5. **Brand assets:** IRCSD has a formal brand guide. For the others, screenshot logos from their websites or request vector files from the district.

6. **Facebook as reference:** Browse all 4 Facebook pages' photo tabs for inspiration and to identify specific images worth requesting permission for.
