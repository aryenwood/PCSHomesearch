North Country Living

Discover the North Country

Fort Drum is more than a duty station. From the Thousand Islands to the Adirondacks — explore everything that makes this area worth calling home.

Get Outside
The North Country is an outdoor playground
Thousand Islands

1,864 islands spanning the US-Canada border. World-class fishing, kayaking, and the iconic Boldt Castle.

1 hr south
Adirondack Park

6 million acres of wilderness. Hiking, camping, and some of the best fall foliage in the Northeast.

1.5 hrs east
Tug Hill Plateau

The snowiest region east of the Rockies. Snowmobiling, cross-country skiing, and 300+ inches of snow per year.

30 min south
Lake Ontario

Charter fishing for salmon and trout. Henderson Harbor and Sackets Harbor are local favorites.

30 min west
Family Friendly
Plenty to keep the family busy
⚓

Sackets Harbor

Historic village on Lake Ontario. Battlefield tours, breweries, galleries, and the annual Can-Am Festival.

🏅

Fort Drum MWR

Outdoor rec, pools, bowling, youth sports — all on post. Don't sleep on the ski lodge at Whiteface.

🎡

Watertown Events

First Fridays downtown, Jefferson County Fair, Salmon Run Mall, plus a growing food scene.

⛵

Alex Bay & Clayton

Summer vacation towns on the St. Lawrence. Boat tours, ice cream shops, and the Antique Boat Museum.

Get Away
Close enough for a weekend

Ottawa, Canada

2 hours north. World-class museums, Rideau Canal, ByWard Market, and NHL hockey.

2 hr drive

Montreal, Canada

2.5 hours north. Old Montreal, incredible food scene, festivals year-round.

2.5 hr drive

Syracuse, NY

90 minutes south. Destiny USA mega-mall, Syracuse University gamedays, and the closest major airport.

90 min drive
